# Privacy Policy for StudyLess

**Last Updated:** December 17, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to StudyLess ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** - Used for authentication and account recovery
- **Account credentials** - Encrypted password for secure access
- **User profile data** - Optional information like name and school

### Study Content

We collect and store:
- **Flashcard content** - Questions and answers you create or generate
- **Study decks** - Your organization of flashcards into subjects
- **Study progress** - Information about your review sessions and performance
- **Images and documents** - Photos and files you upload to generate flashcards

### Usage Data

We automatically collect:
- **Device information** - Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **App usage** - Features used, screens viewed, time spent in app
- **Performance data** - App crashes, errors, and technical diagnostics
- **Analytics** - Aggregated data about how you interact with the app

### Camera and Photo Library

When you use our AI flashcard generation feature:
- **Camera access** - To scan physical study materials
- **Photo library access** - To select images for flashcard generation
- **Processed content** - Text extracted from your images

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Services**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Generate AI-powered flashcards
   - Implement spaced repetition scheduling
   - Track your study progress

2. **Improve Our App**
   - Analyze usage patterns
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Develop new features
   - Optimize performance

3. **Communicate With You**
   - Send study reminders (if you opt in)
   - Provide customer support
   - Send important updates about the service

4. **Ensure Security**
   - Detect and prevent fraud
   - Protect against unauthorized access
   - Maintain data integrity

## How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share your information with:

### Service Providers

- **Authentication services** (Supabase) - Secure account management
- **Analytics providers** (Mixpanel) - Usage analytics and app improvement
- **AI services** (Google Gemini) - Flashcard generation from images
- **Cloud storage** (Supabase) - Secure data storage

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to lawful requests from authorities
- Protect our rights and safety
- Enforce our Terms of Service

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure password hashing
- Protected database access
- Regular security audits

### Data Location

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers provided by Supabase, which uses industry-leading security practices.
